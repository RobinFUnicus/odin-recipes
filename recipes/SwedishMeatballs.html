Swedish Meatballs

Description

The Swedish speciallity. A simple dish that you can find in IKEA.

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Meatballs
  • Brown sauce
  • Lingonberry

Steps

  1. Boil the potato
  2. Prepare the meatballs in your frying pan (what ever that is called in English (it's not frying that is for sure))
  3. Mash the potato
  4. Serve with some lingonberry and brown sauce
  5. Enjoy!
