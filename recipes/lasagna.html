Lasagna

Description

You want to start by placing a pasta plate and then add some meat and some sauce, and then repeat either three or four times depending on how thick you want your lasagna. Finish by putting some cheese on top then you put it in the oven and wait until it gets a golden colour.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pasta plates
  • Strimmed meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Read the whole instruction before you start
  2. Set the oven on 250* Celsius
  3. Do what it says in the description
